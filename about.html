Vineyard

About Us

Since 1998, Vineyard (previously known as Funcity) has given hundreds of users the opportunity to run their own online services. Our goal is to offer shell accounts to people who may not have access to one otherwise. With this shell account, users can create MUDs, talkers, web pages, or many other online applications.
